POKEMON! GOTTA CATCH 'EM ALL!

ASH KETCHUM

He was born in pallet town in Kanto. Since his childhood he loved pokemon and wanted to be Pokemon Master. He travelled through various various regions catching many pokemon, befriending many people, challenging many gym leaders, and battling in various leagues. In Galar, he participated in World Coronation Series(WCS) the largest pokemon league in the world where the 1000+ trainers participate and battle other trainers to increase their ranks and the top 8 people battle as masters 8 in wyndem stadium. They are- rank 1: Leon,2: Cynthia, 3: Steven Stone, 4: Lance, 5: Diantha, 6: Alain, 7: Iris, 8: Ash. Ash defeated them all and got the title of Monarch, the strongest trainer in the world, finally fulfilling his dream.

SOME OF HIS MAJOR POKEMONS:

PIKACHU

It was Ash's starter pokemon whom he loves a lot. At the starting of their journey, pikachu refused to listen to Ash but later he started to listen to him and the duo became best friends.

CHARIZARD

Ash caught charizard when it was a charmander and was abandoned by its old trainer. It was left by its trainer in heavy rain and ash and his team saw it with its tailflame barely burning.It was then took to pokemon center where nurse joy took care of it and then he was with the team during their kanto adventures and then lives with proffeser oak.

GRENINJA

It was given to Ash by Proffeser Syccamore as a froakie. They together travelled in Kalos battling trainers and gym leaders. When it was a frogadier, they went to challenge Olivia, the 7th gym leader in Kalos who is a phychic type expert and can see the future, saw their future and told ash that he and frogadier will cross limits becoming strong and their bond will reach new heights.When it evolved into greninja, their bond became so strong that due to their bond greninja turns into ash greninja, named by Bonnie, which is so strong that they almost defeated the champion, Diantha.

GARY OAK

He was ash's rival in kanto and johto who is also the grandson of Proffeser Oak. He mocks ash everytime and teases him. Before becoming a trainer, they both were roommates and grew together.Now, he has became a pokemon researcher and later, he joined Project Mew, whose main motto is to research on the pokemon mew, with Goh and Horris.

LEON

He was the world champion or the monarch of world coronation series before Ash defeated him.He defeated the former monarch Mustard to become the champion and now, he has became a celebrity and every trainer wants to challenge and become the monarch.

BROCK

He was Ash's companion during his adventures in Kanto,Johto,Hoenn and Sinnoh. He is also Ash's best friend and mentor.He is a great chef and wants to become a pokemon breeder in future.He was the former gym leader of Pewter Gym before his father came back to handle the gym and brock can finally go after his dreams.
